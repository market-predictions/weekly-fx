## 5. Key risks / invalidators

- A renewed oil spike or collapse in diplomacy would quickly restore a broader **USD** defensive premium.
- A fresh U.S. inflation shock would weaken the case for further reducing **USD** cash.
- A more credible BoJ repricing or sharper rise in Japanese yields would improve **JPY** faster than this report assumes.
- A new global growth scare would hit **AUD**, **MXN**, **EUR**, **GBP**, and **NZD** harder than **CHF** and **USD** cash.
- A more dovish-than-expected BoC or BoE pivot would weaken the current constructive read on **CAD** or the improved technical case in **GBP**.
- A cleaner-than-expected geopolitical de-escalation would leave the current **USD** cash weight still too high.
